Welcome to the Clinical Calculator

CC is used to calculate certain health indication values, and a patient's risk of developing a certain disease or illness. It contains calculators for Body Mass Index (BMI), the risk of DVT (deep vein thrombosis) and also a chart to show which growth centile contains a child's height.

To use CC simply choose a calculator from the navigation bar along the top of the page. If you would like to find out more about how the app works and details of who created it visit the "about" page. Or to get in touch with the developers choose the "contact" page.
